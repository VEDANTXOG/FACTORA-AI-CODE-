How It Works

  • ◇ Checks Sources: Checks your given article from reputed sources, trusted news outlets all around the world.
  • ◇ Provide Verdict: Gives you a clear verdict like Real, Likely, Fake or Suspicious.
  • ◇ Learn While Checking: Offers insight into why the article may be misleading and highlights primary signals.
  • WHAT ARE: ◇ Manipulation Score

    What it is: Measures how much article uses misleading language.

    • Analyses emotional words, urgency, exaggeration, fake authority, etc.

    Score Range: 0 - 100%

    • 0-30%: Minimal manipulation

    • 40-60%: Some manipulation (Stay cautious)

    • 70-100%: High manipulation

  • ◇ Verification Score:

    What it is: Measures how much Factora AI can trust the news based on source reliability and contextual evidence.

    • Checks for respectable sources, authority phrases and factual reporting cues.

    Score Range: 0 - 100%

    • 0-30%: Low Confidence

    • 40-60%: Medium Confidence

    • 70-100%: High confidence

  • Primary Signal: Shows the main type of manipulation or attention catching techniques detected.

Educational Mode

Purpose: Awareness and learning mode for students or curious users.

What It is: FACTORA AI's educational mode teaches how to correctly assess news.

Features:

  • • Explains fake news and how it spreads with examples.
  • • Highlights misinformation patterns (emotional, exaggeration, urgency, fake authorities).
  • • Teaches verification steps.
  • • Daily news for students + verification leaderboard (game-like learning).
