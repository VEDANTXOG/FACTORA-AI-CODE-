How It Works

  • 1 Factora AI Overview

    Factora AI is your personal truth detector. Trained on millions of news patterns, it dissects articles for manipulation techniques, source credibility, and factual signals. Backend powered by Django analyzer using NLP models for real-time verdicts.

  • 2 Analysis Process

    Step 1: You paste news → Frontend sends to /analyze/ API.

    Step 2: Backend (news_analyzer/views.py) processes text: source matching, lang analysis, credibility scoring.

    Step 3: Returns verdict, scores, reasons, category in JSON.

    Step 4: UI renders verdict badge, stat bars, explanations.

  • 3 Verdict Types

    • Real (Green): High credibility, reputable sources, factual lang. 🟢

    • Likely Real: Good signals but some uncertainty. 🟡

    • Suspicious: Mixed signals, watch scores. ⚠️

    • Fake (Red): Manipulation detected. 🔴

  • 4 ◇ Manipulation Score (Detailed)

    Detects: Emotional triggers ("crisis!"), urgency ("now!"), exaggeration ("biggest ever"), fake authority ("experts say").

    Example: "SHOCKING: World ends tomorrow!" → 95% manipulation.

    0-30%: Clean • 70%+: Highly suspect.

  • 5 ◇ Credibility Score (Detailed)

    Verifies: Known outlets (BBC, Reuters), fact-check links, balanced phrasing.

    Example: "Per Reuters: ..." → 92% credibility.

    70%+: Trustworthy • <30%:< /strong> Dubious source.

  • 6 Primary Signal + Category

    Flags top issue: "Emotional Bait", "Source Unknown", "Exaggeration". Category: Politics, Health, Tech, etc.

  • 7 Usage Tips

    • Paste full article or headline + link.

    • Save reliable analysis (⭐ button).

    • Educational mode teaches patterns.

    • Always cross-verify critical news.

  • 8 Educational Mode

    Coming: Interactive lessons, daily challenges, leaderboard. Learn to spot fakes like a pro! 🎓
